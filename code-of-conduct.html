Our Code of Conduct

As a community, there are some rules we need to follow.

All our members need to agree to the Code of Conduct:

  1. I shall be respectful of others
  2. I shall be open to collaboration and foster a collaborative environment
  3. I shall not harass, be offensive, or cause any discomfort to others
  4. I shall not commit any act of plagiarism
  5. I shall not commit or be party to any unlawful or unethical act
  6. I agree to be removed from the community if I violate any of the code of conduct or engage in bad behavior

In the end it is up to us, the HATS moderators, to define bad behavior. If you are engaging in bad behavior (e.g. sexually harassing others, posting offensive content or to discriminate against a gender), you may be given a warning that your behavior is not acceptable or be removed from the community.

Report any violations to report@hats.sg or https://goo.gl/forms/28exDfR76rgCsnjR2
